Lava Lamp Animation

For this Lava Lamp animation, I chose to take a very bold approach with this design. I was sitting in myboss's office, trying to find old photos to animate and I looked up and saw his Lava Lamp. I took aphoto of it immediately and edited its' colors on Lightroom. I then went to Photoshop and used the morphing techniques to make the still photo come to life.

Spinning Vinyl Animation

My third animation, the spinning vinyl, was something I had been thinking of for a while. I've been wanting to build a vinyl store website and I wanted the homepage anination to be a spinning vinyl. I found a photo of a vinyl that I've deocrated and began working. I used photoshop to postion the vinyl and create a 360 degree animation.

Stained Glass Animation

My second animation I've created was this stained glass animation effect. I love when things deal with changing colors, so I thought "Why not incorporate it?" This process involved selecting each piece of the window on Photoshop and changing the color of that piece. I recorded a new transition every time that I changed the color.
