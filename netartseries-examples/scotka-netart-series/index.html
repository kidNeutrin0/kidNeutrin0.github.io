Cosmic Currency: The Timeless Dance of Wealth and Time

Drifting through the vast expanse, time and money weave their silent dance. Galaxies spin, untouched by gold, yet fortunes fade as moments unfold. A second lost, a dollar spent—both vanish, never to be lent. The clock hands turn, a measured beat, ticking wealth and time’s retreat. Stars burn bright and then decay, like coins exchanged and cast away. Is wealth the measure, or is it time? In endless space, both seem sublime.
